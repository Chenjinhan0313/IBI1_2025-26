The most important thing I learned is the difference between np.random.choice() and np.random.random()
np.random.choice() is best used when I need to randomly sample elements from a given 1D array,optionally with specified probabilities.
np.random.random() is best used when I need to generate random floating-point numbers uniformly distributed in the interval[0.0,1.0]
